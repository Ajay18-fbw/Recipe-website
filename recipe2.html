Classic Pasta Carbonara

A creamy Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, and crispy bacon. Quick and irresistible!

Prep Time
5 mins
Cook Time
20 mins
Total Time
25 mins
Servings
2 people
Difficulty
Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti or fettuccine
  • 100g bacon or pancetta, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 egg yolk (extra creaminess)
  • 60g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. 1
    Boil pasta: Cook spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente (check packet instructions). Save 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. 2
    Cook bacon: While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add bacon and garlic. Cook until bacon is golden and crispy (about 5 minutes).
  3. 3
    Make the sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together eggs, egg yolk, and grated Parmesan. Add a pinch of black pepper. Mix well.
  4. 4
    Combine everything: Remove the bacon pan from heat. Add the drained pasta to the pan and toss quickly. Pour the egg mixture over the pasta and toss continuously.
  5. 5
    Adjust consistency: Add a splash of reserved pasta water to loosen the sauce if needed. The heat from the pasta will gently cook the eggs into a creamy sauce. Do not overheat or eggs will scramble.
  6. 6
    Serve immediately: Plate the pasta, top with extra Parmesan, freshly cracked pepper, and parsley. Serve right away while hot.
