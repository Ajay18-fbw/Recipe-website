Creamy Butter Chicken

A rich ,aromatic North Indian curry that's loved worldwide .

Prep Time
15 mins
Cook Time
30 mins
Total Time
45 mins
Servings
4 people
Difficulty
Medium

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 400g crushed tomatoes
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. 1
    Marinate the chicken: Mix yogurt, chilli powder, garam masala, and salt. Add chicken and mix well. Marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. 2
    Cook the chicken: Heat butter in a pan. Add chicken and cook for 8–10 minutes until slightly charred. Remove and set aside.
  3. 3
    Make the gravy: In same pan, add cumin seeds. Add onions and cook until golden. Add garlic and ginger, cook 1 more minute.
  4. 4
    Add tomatoes: Pour in crushed tomatoes and chilli powder. Cook on medium heat for 10 minutes until oil separates.
  5. 5
    Add cream: Lower the heat. Add heavy cream, stir gently. Add cooked chicken back. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. 6
    Serve: Garnish with coriander and cream drizzle. Serve hot with naan or rice!
