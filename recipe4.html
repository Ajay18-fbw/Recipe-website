Veggie Fried Rice

A colorful, healthy, and super quick stir-fried rice loaded with fresh vegetables. Ready in 20 minutes!

Prep Time
5 mins
Cook Time
15 mins
Total Time
20 mins
Servings
3 people
Difficulty
Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (day-old rice works best)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, corn, beans)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 2 eggs (optional — skip for vegan)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • Spring onions (green part) for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. 1
    Prepare everything first: Chop all vegetables and have them ready. Fried rice cooks very fast, so you need all ingredients at hand before you start.
  2. 2
    Scramble eggs: Heat 1 tbsp oil in a large wok or pan over high heat. Beat eggs and pour them in. Scramble quickly and remove them. Set aside. (Skip this step for vegan version.)
  3. 3
    Stir-fry veggies: Add remaining oil to the pan. Add garlic and ginger — stir for 30 seconds. Add onion and stir-fry for 2 minutes. Add mixed vegetables and cook on high heat for 3–4 minutes.
  4. 4
    Add rice: Add the cooked rice to the wok. Break any lumps with a spatula. Toss everything together on high heat for 2–3 minutes. The rice should get slightly crispy.
  5. 5
    Season it: Add soy sauce, sesame oil, black pepper, and salt. Mix well and toss. Add the scrambled eggs back and stir everything together.
  6. 6
    Serve hot: Transfer to a bowl, garnish with chopped spring onions. Serve immediately. Pairs great with a fried egg on top or with soy dipping sauce on the side.
