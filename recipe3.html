Chocolate Lava Cake

A warm chocolate cake with a gooey molten center. Restaurant-quality dessert made at home!

Prep Time
10 mins
Cook Time
12 mins
Total Time
22 mins
Servings
4 cakes
Difficulty
Medium

Ingredients

  • 200g dark chocolate (70% cocoa), chopped
  • 100g unsalted butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 3 large eggs
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 80g caster sugar
  • 30g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • Cocoa powder for dusting moulds
  • Vanilla ice cream to serve (optional but recommended)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. 1
    Preheat oven: Set your oven to 200°C (180°C fan). Grease 4 ramekins or small oven-safe cups with butter and dust with cocoa powder. This stops the cake from sticking.
  2. 2
    Melt chocolate and butter: Place chopped chocolate and butter in a heatproof bowl over a pot of simmering water (double boiler method). Stir until completely melted and smooth. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
  3. 3
    Whisk eggs: In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, egg yolks, and caster sugar until the mixture becomes pale and slightly thick (about 2 minutes).
  4. 4
    Combine: Pour the melted chocolate mixture into the egg mixture. Add vanilla extract and a pinch of salt. Stir gently. Fold in the flour until just combined — don't overmix.
  5. 5
    Fill and bake: Pour the batter evenly into the prepared ramekins. Bake in the oven for exactly 10–12 minutes. The edges should be set but the center should still jiggle slightly.
  6. 6
    Serve immediately: Let rest for 1 minute. Run a knife around the edge, place a plate on top, and carefully flip to unmould. Serve immediately with a scoop of vanilla ice cream!
